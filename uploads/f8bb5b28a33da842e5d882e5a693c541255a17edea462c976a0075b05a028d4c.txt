
    COMPANY SECURITY POLICY v1.0
    
    Section 1: Password Requirements
    All employees must use strong passwords that meet the following criteria:
    - Minimum 12 characters in length
    - Must contain uppercase and lowercase letters
    - Must contain at least one number
    - Must contain at least one special character
    - Passwords must be changed every 90 days
    - Previous passwords cannot be reused for 12 months
    
    Section 2: Data Access Controls
    - Access to sensitive data requires manager approval
    - All data access must be logged and monitored
    - Employees must not share their login credentials
    - Remote access requires VPN connection
    - Mobile devices must be encrypted
    
    Section 3: Incident Response
    - Security incidents must be reported within 1 hour
    - All incidents must be documented in the incident management system
    - Critical incidents require immediate escalation to management
    - Post-incident reviews must be conducted within 7 days
    
    Section 4: Data Classification
    - Public: Information that can be freely shared
    - Internal: Information for company use only
    - Confidential: Sensitive business information
    - Restricted: Highly sensitive information requiring special handling
